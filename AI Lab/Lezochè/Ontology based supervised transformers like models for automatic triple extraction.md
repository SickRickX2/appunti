Not always a good percent of success in a domain is good in another domain, i.e. In medical domain 95% accuracy is not really good.
In financial is even worse! 

Let s move in a manufactury domain, we have to control the engine quality and the 5% percent is not good. In that case we lose the contract.

So for us it's ok to use with 95% rate of success but only in simple cases not important for social and industrial context.

We have to shrink as possible the result or trying to get something for us to understand that the result is not entirely trustful.
